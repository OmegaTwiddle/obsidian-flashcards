This code repository is designed to be a pack of flashcards suited for use in the [Spaced Repetition](https://github.com/st3v3nmw/obsidian-spaced-repetition) Obsidian community plugin.

## Instructions
To use, simply download this repository, or your preferred flashcard pack. Then open up your Obsidian vault (File > Open Vault > three dots ... > Reveal vault in Finder). Lastly, drag the flashcard pack(s) into your Obsidian vault. For more details on how to use the Spaced Repetition plugin, refer to their [user manual](https://www.stephenmwangi.com/obsidian-spaced-repetition/).


## Contributions
